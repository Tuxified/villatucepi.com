Tučepi is one of the most attractive and visited towns of Makarska riviera and a very popular touristic destination for Croatian and European tourists.

Located below the majestic mountain Biokovo, Tučepi is known for having the longest beach of the Makarska riviera (more than 4 km) and a beautiful promenade with pine trees.

Because of a beautiful and breathtaking landscape, the Biokovo Nature Park is a perfect place for those who love nature, hiking and cycling.

Located in central Dalmatia and only 40 km away from the highway A1, Tučepi is connected  by road to whole of Europe.

By sea through Makarska, Split and Drvenik Tučepi is connected with the central Dalmatian islands (Brač, Hvar, Korčula, Lastovo and Vis) and also with Italy by direct ferry connections from Split to Ancona, Pescara to Zadar and Dubrovnik to Bari .

The Split airport is 80 km distant from Tučepi and is connected to all major European cities. Distances:

  • Makarska - 2 km
  • Split - 65 km
  • Dubrovnik - 190 km
  • Zagreb - 455 km
